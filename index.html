About Us

The Stardust Discovery Project embodies a multifaceted approach to fostering scientific literacy and career advancement within Africa. Firstly, the project aims to support STEM/Physics students and educators in navigating the content and certificate examinations by providing targeted coaching and resources, while also offering guidance to their educators on effective teaching methodologies. Secondly, through engaging science broadcasts, the project endeavors to elucidate natural phenomena using accessible language, ensuring comprehension for audiences of all backgrounds. Lastly, by providing STEM-based career coaching and advising, particularly to young individuals, the project seeks to nurture the next generation of scientific talents. Through these initiatives, the Stardust Discovery Project endeavors to inspire curiosity, empower communities, and pave the way for a brighter scientific future in Africa while making science accessible to all.

Science Broadcast

Through engaging science broadcasts, the project aims to elucidate natural phenomena using accessible language, thereby highlighting the connection between astronomy and daily life. It seeks to improve our standard of living through scientific knowledge, foster better responses to events by applying scientific principles, and deepen our understanding of humanity's place in the universe. Additionally, the project promotes mutual appreciation of our origins through science, transcending race and color, and encourages recognition of the goodness and might of God.

Science Teacher Consulting

The primary goal of the physics educational consulting aspect of Stardust Discovery is to elevate the quality of physics education in Africa by offering expertise, resources, and support to educational institutions and stakeholders. We conduct workshops for physics and STEM educators to enhance their teaching skills, update them with the latest pedagogical techniques, and incorporate innovative, student-centered teaching methods, such as inquiry-based learning. Additionally, we assist institutions in integrating educational technologies like simulations, virtual laboratories, and online learning platforms to boost student engagement and learning outcomes. We also conduct evaluations and assessments of physics programs to ensure they meet educational standards and objectives, providing recommendations for continuous improvement.

STEM Career Coaching

We support students through this critical phase of their lives by providing comprehensive services, including career advice and coaching. We help students define their future educational aspirations and evaluate the general selection requirements, prospects, and opportunities available to them. Our guidance extends to selecting countries, schools, and courses, and we provide detailed information on the schools chosen by the student. We discuss the eligibility criteria and requirements for each selected school, review transcripts, statements, resumes, and other documents, and assess the student's past academic records and experiences. Based on this comprehensive review, we offer tailored recommendations to align with the student's academic history and goals.
